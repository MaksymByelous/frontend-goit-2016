About us

We are the team of enthusiasts who wants to make this world better. Our team unites professionals from different spheres: business, journalism, software development and engineering. We have gathered because we are united by one aim - using state-of-the-art Natural Language Processing to create an automated system that identifies facts and claims and instantly searches databases to verify or invalidate.
research directions
  • Sources management: create database of curated information useful to the interest and needs of potential customers and users
  • Claim detection: highlighting the text that represents a checkable assertion
  • Claim representation: translating claims into machine-readable form
  • Claim verification: true or false
short-term goals
To create a basic facts knowledge base and develop claims detection algorithms, as well as create various ways of claims representation and verification.
long-term goals
stage 1
Approach it as an IR problem: detect claims and find relevant content among the already created documents (news, fact databases).
stage 2
Determine claim validity by matching with the relevant sources and applying resolution strategies according to source trustworthiness.
stage 3
Handling of indirect quotes and complicated claims using an abstract representation invariant to paraphrasing and other distortions.
